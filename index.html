The Vision Behind RegulaCore Solutions

Sri Lakshmi built RegulaCore Solutions from direct experience working as an Assistant Accountant at KBM Chartered Certified Accountants and Registered Auditors in the UK, where she witnessed firsthand the compliance challenges that SME founders face daily.

Having personally handled VAT returns, PAYE filings, FPS and EPS submissions to HMRC, pension auto-enrolment, and statutory audit support, she identified the critical gap: SMEs have accounting tools but no compliance governance layer. RegulaCore Solutions was designed to fill that gap.
